## Quick Bio

Maria Elena Santos is a lifelong Coloradan, housing policy expert, and community advocate running to represent House District 42. The daughter of Mexican immigrants, Maria was the first in her family to attend college. She has spent 20+ years fighting for working families—and she's ready to take that fight to the State House.

---

## Professional Background

- **Executive Director**, Colorado Nonprofit Housing Coalition (2018–Present)
  - Leads statewide affordable housing advocacy
  - Manages $2.4M annual budget
  - Coordinates 45 member organizations

- **Deputy Director**, Aurora Housing Authority (2012–2018)
  - Oversaw 1,200 affordable housing units
  - Managed $8M annual budget
  - Led energy efficiency retrofits saving $340K annually

- **Policy Analyst**, Colorado Department of Local Affairs (2005–2012)
  - Housing policy research and legislative drafting
  - Testified before state legislature

- **Community Organizer**, Mi Casa Resource Center (2000–2005)
  - Connected immigrant families with housing, jobs, and education

## Top 3 Priorities

### 1. Affordable Housing
Expand housing supply, protect tenants from displacement, and provide emergency assistance to families in crisis.

### 2. Quality Education
Fully fund public schools, raise teacher pay to $50K starting salary, and expand pre-K access.

### 3. Healthcare Access
Lower costs through Colorado Option, cap insulin prices, and expand mental health services.
