Press Releases

September 5, 2024

Santos Proposes Education Funding Overhaul

Maria Santos today released her full education platform, "Investing in Our Future," calling for Colorado to reach top-25 status in per-pupil funding within five years. The plan proposes $50,000 starting salaries for teachers, universal pre-K for all 3-year-olds, and mental health counselors at a 1:250 student ratio. Santos received the endorsement of the Colorado Education Association at the announcement event.

August 12, 2024

Colorado Nurses Association Endorses Santos

The Colorado Nurses Association today announced its endorsement of Maria Santos for State Representative in House District 42. "Maria Santos understands the challenges healthcare workers face and has a real plan to improve our healthcare system," said CNA President Jennifer Martinez. "She's the leader we need in the State House." Santos outlined her healthcare platform, including support for the Colorado Option and expanded mental health services.

July 1, 2024

Santos Releases Comprehensive Housing Plan

Maria Santos today released "A Home for Every Family," a comprehensive housing plan addressing Colorado's affordable housing crisis. The plan calls for a 40% increase in state Low Income Housing Tax Credit allocation, creation of a $50M Colorado Housing Trust Fund, and statewide just-cause eviction protections. "Housing costs are pushing families out of the communities they've called home for generations," Santos said. "This plan offers real solutions — not just rhetoric."

June 15, 2024

Maria Santos Announces Candidacy for HD-42

Maria Elena Santos, a housing advocate and nonprofit leader with over 20 years of public service experience, today announced her candidacy for State Representative in House District 42. "After 20 years fighting for working families, I'm ready to take that fight to the State House," Santos said at a campaign launch rally attended by over 250 supporters. Santos received endorsements from State Senator Angela Williams and Aurora Mayor Mike Coffman at the event.
