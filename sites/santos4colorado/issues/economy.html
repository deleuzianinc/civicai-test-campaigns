Economy & Jobs

Building an economy that works for working families

The Challenge

Wages haven't kept pace with costs. Small businesses struggle to compete with corporate giants. Workers in the gig economy lack basic protections. The economy is growing, but not everyone is benefiting — too many families are working harder than ever and still falling behind.

My Position

I will fight for an economy that rewards work — through fair wages, workforce development, and support for small businesses and entrepreneurs in every community.

Key Priorities

  • Support gradual minimum wage increase to $18/hour by 2028 — Ensure work pays enough to live on. A full-time worker shouldn't need food stamps.
  • Expand apprenticeship programs — Construction, healthcare, and technology pathways to good careers. Not everyone needs a four-year degree to succeed.
  • Create $25M small business grant fund — Support entrepreneurs in underserved communities who lack access to traditional capital.
  • Support paid family and medical leave expansion — Care for family without losing your job. No one should have to choose between their paycheck and their loved ones.
  • Invest in broadband infrastructure — Connect rural and underserved areas so every Coloradan can participate in the modern economy.
  • Protect gig workers — Portable benefits for the modern economy. Uber drivers and freelancers deserve basic protections too.

My Approach

My parents worked multiple jobs their entire lives and still struggled. That's wrong. Work should pay enough to live on.

I believe in an economy that creates pathways to middle-class stability. Not everyone needs a four-year degree — but everyone deserves access to training that leads to good-paying careers. Apprenticeship programs in construction, healthcare, and technology can transform lives and fill critical workforce gaps.

Small businesses are the backbone of our communities, but too many entrepreneurs in historically underserved neighborhoods lack access to capital. A targeted grant fund can unlock opportunity for business owners who've been left behind by traditional lending.

And we need to catch up to the modern economy. Gig workers deserve protections. Remote work requires broadband. Paid leave means you shouldn't have to choose between caring for your family and keeping your job.
